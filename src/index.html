About us
About us
In this day and age where knowledge about computers, their working and programming are like weapons that you must have in your arsenal, it is vital for us all to atleast have an insight in this intricate craft. "Computer Club" is an effort from the department of Computer Engineering, SGSITS to provide an opportunity for every student of the college to develop a level of proficiency in this field. Our mission is to give every student of the college a competitive stage to show their mettle and more importantly develop their sills in the ever growing world of computers.
